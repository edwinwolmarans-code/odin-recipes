Mushroom Pasta

Description

Delight your family and friends with this simple tasty dish.

Prefected by generations of Italian mums, this four ingredent dish is ready to satisfy tastebuds the world over in just 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet penne pasta
  • 1 packet sliced mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat and stir in sliced mushrooms. Cook and stir until the mushrooms have softened and released their liquid. Add the minced garlic, and cook 2 more minutes.
  3. To serve, toss the cooked penne with the msuhrooms, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
